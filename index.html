Electronics and Embedded Systems

An embedded system is a set of hardware and software designed to carry out a predefined task. Embedded systems are designed for a specific task. Although they use computer techniques, they cannot be used as a general purpose computer using a variety of different programmes for different task. In this way their function can be focussed onto what they need to do, and they can accordingly be made cheaper and more efficiently.

Fabrication

Fabrication is distinct from other manufacturing processes. Fabrication manufacturing processes differ depending on the material and the desired end product. The process can be used in making mass-produced products or custom designs.
